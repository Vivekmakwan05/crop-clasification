why choose us?

Kharif season is the monsoon or rainy season in agriculture, typically running from June to October. During this period, crops like rice, millet, and cotton are sown and harvested. Kharif crops depend on the rainfall for irrigation and are well-suited to the wet and warm conditions of the monsoon season.
